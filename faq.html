Frequently Asked Questions

   
                 
       

Use your HZ student card at designated printing stations around campus. Upload your files to the HZ print service and confirm printing with your card.

     
   
   
                 
       

Use scanning facilities at multimedia library and Open Learning Centre. You can scan documents to USB, email, or cloud storage accessible from your laptop.

     
   
   
                 
       

Access the HZ webshop through the My.HZ portal. Payment is accepted via student card balance or other provided payment methods.

     
   
   
                 
       

Book project spaces via the internal HZ portal or by contacting facility management. Reservations may be required in advance for popular spaces.

     
   
   
                 
       

An HZ parking permit or guest pass is required. Use your student card for access. Adhere to parking rules and designated zones.
